Synchronise your openScale weight data to various clouds.

Features:
* automatic synchronization to Google Fit, wger and/or MQTT 3.1 when inserting, editing, deleting in openScale
* manual complete synchronization from openScale to Google Fit, wger and/or MQTT 3.1
* background service which is only called when needed (low battery consumption)
* selectable openScale user to synchronize with
* easy to use graphical user interface
